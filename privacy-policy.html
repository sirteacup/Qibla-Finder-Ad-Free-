Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Qibla Finder ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App requests access to your device's precise and approximate location (via ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION permissions). This location data is used solely to calculate the direction of the Qibla (the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca) relative to your current position.

Your location data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted, stored, or shared with us or any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To determine your geographic coordinates and compute the Qibla bearing.
  • To display compass-based directional guidance within the App.

We do not use your location for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the above.

3. Data Storage and Retention

The App does not collect, store, or retain any personal data. No data is written to external servers or databases. Location information is used in real time and discarded when the App is closed or the permission is revoked.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Permissions

  • Location (Fine & Coarse): Required to calculate the Qibla direction. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings. Without it, the App cannot determine your position and the Qibla direction will not be available.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, this App is safe for general audiences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
